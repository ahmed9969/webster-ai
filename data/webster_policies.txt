WEBSTER UNIVERSITY TASHKENT - ACADEMIC POLICIES

CREDIT LOAD LIMITS PER SEMESTER:
- First semester students (new/freshman): Maximum 13 credits
- Students with GPA 3.0 or above: Maximum 18 credits
- Students with GPA below 3.0: Maximum 16 credits
- Students on Academic Probation: Maximum 12 credits
- Summer term: Maximum 9 credits

MINIMUM CREDIT LOAD:
- Full-time students: Minimum 12 credits per semester
- Part-time students: Less than 12 credits per semester
- Financial aid recipients must maintain full-time status (12+ credits)

OVERLOAD POLICY:
- Taking more than 18 credits requires written approval from Academic Dean
- Overload requests considered only for students with GPA 3.5 or above

GRADUATION REQUIREMENTS:
- Total credits required: 128 credits minimum for most bachelor's degrees
- Minimum GPA for graduation: 2.0 overall GPA
- Must complete all required major courses
- Must complete Global Citizenship Program (GCP) requirements
- Must complete at least 45 credits at Webster University (residency requirement)

GPA REQUIREMENTS:
- Good Academic Standing: GPA 2.0 or above
- Academic Warning: GPA between 1.5 and 1.99
- Academic Probation: GPA below 1.5
- Dean's List: GPA 3.5 or above in a semester with 12+ credits

GRADING SCALE:
A = 4.0 (93-100%)
A- = 3.7 (90-92%)
B+ = 3.3 (87-89%)
B = 3.0 (83-86%)
B- = 2.7 (80-82%)
C+ = 2.3 (77-79%)
C = 2.0 (73-76%)
C- = 1.7 (70-72%)
D+ = 1.3 (67-69%)
D = 1.0 (60-66%)
F = 0.0 (below 60%)

WITHDRAWAL POLICY:
- Students may drop courses before the drop deadline with no academic record
- Students may withdraw from courses before withdrawal deadline with W on transcript
- Withdrawing from all courses counts as university withdrawal

ATTENDANCE POLICY:
- Regular attendance is expected in all courses
- Professors set their own attendance policies in course syllabus
- Excessive absences may result in grade penalty or course failure

ACADEMIC CALENDAR STRUCTURE:
- Fall Semester: August to December
- Spring Semester: January to May
- Summer Term: May to August
- Each semester has two 8-week terms (undergraduate) within the full semester

TRANSFER CREDITS:
- Maximum 64 transfer credits accepted from community colleges
- Maximum 90 transfer credits accepted from 4-year institutions
- Minimum grade of C required for transfer credit acceptance
- Transfer credits evaluated by Registrar's Office
