Webster University Online Undergrad Programs
Our students come to us from a variety of backgrounds, each bringing a breadth of
experiences, perspectives, ideas, and academic goals. Since global citizenship and
real-world application are at the core of our mission, we encourage our students to
push the boundaries of their comfort levels, take healthy risks, engage with others,
and increase their understanding of this dynamic world.
Choose the Program That's Right for You
With our web-enhanced classroom options, additional campuses in the United States
and around the world, and five convenient start times per year, our programs reflect
our students’ evolving needs.
And, because we have metropolitan, military, and corporate locations around the world,
our students are immersed in a vibrant and unique learning experience.

“Growing up, I have witnessed many (people) struggle financially, and I knew that I
wanted to help as many people as I could by teaching others to become financially
literate. I will be the first in my family to finish college, and I hope to set an

Specialized Summer Study Abroad: 4 Weeks, 4 Credits
In Summer 2024, Webster University is launching new four-week Study Abroad sessions
at three international campuses: Geneva, Leiden and Vienna.
Summer 1 is June 3-28, and Summer 2 is July 1-26. Each four-week session will host
thematic programs comprised of a three-credit core course and a one-credit experiential
learning course.
